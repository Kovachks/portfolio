About Me

Extremely passionate and motivated Full Stack Web Developer who is always learning new technologies and continuing to master old ones.

While I did not go to school to learn computer programming I have always been extremely interested in computers. I build my own personal computers, enjoy reading about tech news, and learning about the latest technologies. I quit my role as an accountant to enroll in the UNC-Charlotte Coding Bootcamp which I graduated from in October 2017 in order to pursue my dream of becoming a web developer.

As part of the coding bootcamp we were able to work with many different languages and technologies. We started out at the basics of HTML/CSS and worked our way up through React.js and C#. In my free time I continue to hone my skills as a developer.
